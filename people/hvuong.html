Graduate research assistant

Hung Vuong is a Chemical Physics Ph.D student from Hanoi, Vietnam. Before coming to Columbia, he graduated from Grinnell College in Iowa with a B.A. in Mathematics and Chemistry. His research in the Billinge group will be about applying Machine Learning in analyzing PDF data. Outside academics, he's interested in photography and exploring the food scene of NYC.
