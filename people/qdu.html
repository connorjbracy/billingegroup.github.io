Professor

Dr.Du is the Fu Foundation Professor of Applied Mathematics at the Department of Applied Physics and Applied Mathematics (APAM), Fu Foundation School of Engineering and Applied Science (SEAS), Columbia University. He is also an affiliated with the Data Science Institute (DSI). He chaired the Applied Mathematics Program Committee from 2014-2020 and served in DSI as a co-Chair of the Center for Foundations of Data Science from 2017-2019. Currently he also serves as a co-Chair of the Center for Computing Systems for Data-Driven Science.
